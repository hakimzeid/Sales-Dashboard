# Excel Sales Analysis Dashboard 📊

This project contains a **Sales Analysis Dashboard** built entirely in **Microsoft Excel**, designed to provide insights into sales performance across **products, categories, quantities, and regions**. It helps decision-makers track trends, identify top-performing products, and visualize regional sales data.  

---

## **Features**

- **Overall Business Metrics:**  
  - Total Sales, Total Profit, Average Discount, Total Margin  
- **Category Analysis:**  
  - Breakdown of sales, profit, and quantity by category (Technology, Office Supplies, Furniture)  
- **Product Analysis:**  
  - Top 10 products by sales  
  - Bottom 10 products by sales for improvement analysis  
- **Subcategory Insights:**  
  - Total profit and sales by subcategory  
  - Quantity sold by subcategory  
- **Regional Insights:**  
  - Interactive map showing top customers by state  
- **Interactive Filters:**  
  - Filter data by Year, Category, and Quarter for deeper analysis  

---

## **Excel Tools & Features Used**

- **Pivot Tables:** Summarize and analyze data efficiently  
- **Charts & Pie Charts:** Visualize sales and profit distribution  
- **Conditional Formatting:** Highlight key insights and outliers  
- **Slicers & Filters:** Allow interactive exploration of data  
- **Maps:** Display geographic distribution of customers  
- **Combo Charts (Column + Line):** Compare sales and profit in one chart  

---

## **Purpose**

The dashboard is designed to:  

- Provide a **comprehensive view of sales performance**  
- Identify **high-performing products** and **categories**  
- Highlight **underperforming products** for decision-making  
- Enable **geographical analysis** of top customers  
- Serve as a **dynamic reporting tool** for business intelligence  

---


## **Conclusion**

This Excel dashboard demonstrates the power of **Excel for data analysis and visualization**, going beyond traditional spreadsheets to provide actionable business insights.
